To use live Slack data, add the Slack bot token and signing secret to your .env file: 

SLACK_BOT_TOKEN=xoxb-...your-token...
SLACK_SIGNING_SECRET=your-signing-secret

Then restart the app. Make sure the bot token has the required scopes for reading channels, users, histories, and sending messages.

After a successful connection, use Slack itself to interact with the bot: open the workspace, DM the bot, or use a slash command like /jira.
